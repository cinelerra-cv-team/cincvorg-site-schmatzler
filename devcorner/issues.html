Known issues and workarounds

These are bugs we know about, and workarounds for some of them. Workarounds are not fixes! They just allow you to get by, until the bug is fixed.

DV rendering is all green!

Oct. 30, 2003: DV rendering is not SMP safe. It will even fail on a single P4, producing an image that is pure green.
Workaround: In Preferences→Performance, check the "force single processor use" checkbox.

MP3 output is scrambled and full of echo

Oct. 30, 2003: MP3 rendering is currently not SMP safe. It will even fail on a single P4.
Workaround: In Preferences→Performance, check the "force single processor use" checkbox.

In MPEG4 AVIs and MOVs the sound is badly out of synch

No known workaround at this point
