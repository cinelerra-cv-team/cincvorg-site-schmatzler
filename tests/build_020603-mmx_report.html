Source from CVS 02.Jun.2003. Built 08.Jun.2003, with mmx.

Building...

Build system: Laptop with 500MHz AMD K6-2 CPU and 128MB RAM.
mmx or 3DNOW support enabled
Build time: ~45 minutes.

Running...

Playback to ieee1394 has occational dropouts and jitter, and the picture and sound disappears periodically.

Sustained DV playback framerate (X11-XV) is ~7 fps. No significant improvement above the non-mmx build. The viewer and the compositor are not much different.

VCD-compatible MPEG1 playback got much smoother (than without mmx) in the viewer; almost no frames were dropped. In the compositor, it was still a bit jerky: Cinelerra reported 15fps.
